Neuroscience



History of Neuroscience

Ancient to Renaissance: Early references to brain functions in Egyptian and Greek texts; Galen's anatomical studies. 18th-19th Centuries: Discoveries in bioelectricity (Galvani), neuron functions (Cajal, Golgi), and brain localization (Gall). 20th Century to Present: Development of EEG, neurotransmitter discoveries, and modern imaging techniques like MRI and fMRI.
